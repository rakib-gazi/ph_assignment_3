What is the typical timeline for building a custom home?

The timeline for building a custom home can vary depending on the project's complexity, size, and weather conditions. Generally, it takes about 8 to 12 months from start to finish.

Can I make changes to the floor plan during construction?

Yes, modifications can be made during construction, but they may impact the timeline and budget. We recommend finalizing all decisions before construction begins to avoid delays.

What materials do you recommend for energy-efficient homes?

We recommend using high-performance insulation, energy-efficient windows, and sustainable materials like bamboo or recycled wood. Incorporating solar panels and smart home technology can further enhance energy efficiency.
